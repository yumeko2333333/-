伍津玉的奇妙冒险

yume

In the scorching summer, the sunlight falls on the verdant forest, and the gentle breeze brushes against the cheeks, bringing a hint of coolness. At this moment, you stand in front of the magnificent waterfall, feeling the power of the rushing water, listening to the sound of the waterfall pouring down, as if the whole world is immersed in the fresh water vapor. The waterfall is like a flowing painting, with rainbow like mist dancing in the air under the sunlight, so beautiful that it is intoxicating. This summer, let the coolness and beauty of the waterfall become your most unforgettable memories.
